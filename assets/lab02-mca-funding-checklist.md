# Free MCA Funding Checklist
## 7 Steps to Qualify for $50K–$500K in Business Capital

*Use this checklist before applying for Merchant Cash Advance (MCA) funding to maximize your approval odds and get the best terms.*

---

### ✅ Step 1 — Know Your Monthly Revenue
Lenders fund based on your monthly deposits, not your profit.
- [ ] Pull your last **3 months of bank statements**
- [ ] Calculate your **average monthly revenue** (total deposits ÷ 3)
- [ ] Confirm your average is **at least $10,000/month** (most MCA minimums)

> 💡 *Pro tip: Consistent monthly deposits matter more than one big month.*

---

### ✅ Step 2 — Check Your Business Age
- [ ] Confirm your business has been operating for **at least 6 months**
- [ ] Have your **business formation documents** ready (LLC, Corp, DBA filing)
- [ ] Locate your **EIN (Employer Identification Number)**

---

### ✅ Step 3 — Gather Your Bank Statements
This is the #1 document MCA funders review.
- [ ] 3 most recent **complete bank statements** (all pages)
- [ ] Statements must show the **business name** on the account
- [ ] No more than **5 negative balance days** per month (ideally zero)
- [ ] Minimal **NSF (non-sufficient funds) occurrences**

---

### ✅ Step 4 — Understand Your Credit Picture
MCA is not a bank loan — credit scores matter less than cash flow.
- [ ] Know your **personal credit score** (600+ preferred, not required)
- [ ] Check for **open bankruptcies** (active BK = automatic decline at most funders)
- [ ] Note any **existing MCA balances** (stacking limits approval amounts)

---

### ✅ Step 5 — Prepare Your Business Basics
- [ ] **Business name** (exact legal name as registered)
- [ ] **Business address** (physical, not P.O. box)
- [ ] **Industry type** (some industries have restrictions — ask your broker)
- [ ] **Owner's name, DOB, and SSN** (for soft credit pull authorization)
- [ ] **Ownership percentage** (if multiple owners, all 50%+ owners must sign)

---

### ✅ Step 6 — Know What You Need the Money For
Funders approve faster when you have a clear purpose.
- [ ] Write one sentence describing your use of funds
  - *Example: "Purchase new equipment for expanded service capacity"*
  - *Example: "Bridge payroll gap during seasonal slowdown"*
  - *Example: "Pre-purchase inventory for a confirmed large order"*

---

### ✅ Step 7 — Understand the Terms Before You Sign
- [ ] Ask for the **factor rate** (e.g., 1.35 means you repay $1.35 for every $1 borrowed)
- [ ] Confirm the **repayment frequency** (daily or weekly ACH)
- [ ] Calculate your **total payback amount** (funded amount × factor rate)
- [ ] Confirm there is **no prepayment penalty** (or understand the terms if there is)
- [ ] Ask about **renewal eligibility** (many businesses renew at 50–70% payoff)

---

## 📊 Quick Qualification Summary

| Factor | Minimum | Ideal |

| Bank Statements | 3 months | 6 months |

| Credit Score | 550+ | 650+ |
| Active Bankruptcies | None | None |
